About Me

I'm a designer and drafting engineer based in Northern Virginia, currently working for a construction company that specializes in custom cabinetry. My expertise spans a wide spectrum of creative and technical disciplines, including 3D modeling/animation/rendering, CAD, architectural visualization, photography, graphic design, cloud software, and web development using HTML, css, and modern frameworks.

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work with diverse clients on logo design, website development, and social media strategy. My background combines strong marketing acumen with hands-on technical skills, and I'm constantly driven by curiosity, whether it's exploring electrical and mechanical engineering, diving into machine learning ethics, or mastering new creative tools.
